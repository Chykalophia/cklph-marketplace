## [0.1.0] — 2026-05-31

### Added — initial release
- **`rocks`** — quarterly priorities (pick 3-7, define "done", surface misses early).
- **`weekly-sync`** — structured weekly team meeting cadence (issues → discuss → solve, not status theater).
- **`scorecard`** — weekly leading-metrics discipline.
- **`issues-ids`** — Identify → Discuss → Solve loop.
- **`people-fit`** — get-it / want-it / capacity check.
- **`accountability-map`** — role-not-title clarification.
